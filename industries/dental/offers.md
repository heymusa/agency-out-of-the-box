# Offers — Dental Marketing

Typical service offers and pricing ranges for dental marketing agencies in 2026. Used by `offer-design` skill as scaffolding when filling `agency-context.md §6 / §7`.

## Service tier ladder (Brunson value ladder applied to dental)

### Free tier — lead magnet
- **Dental SEO audit deck** (12-15 pages PDF, autogenerated from a domain input + manual review).
- **Local pack visibility scorecard** for the practice's primary geography.
- **2026 dental marketing benchmarks report** (anonymous data from current clients).
- **"$25k Dental Marketing Budget Allocation Workbook"** (interactive tool / spreadsheet).

### Entry tier — paid low-ticket ($1k-$3k one-time)
- **Done-for-you GBP rebuild** ($1,500-$2,500). Single deliverable, 5-7 day turnaround.
- **Reactivation campaign to inactive patients** ($1,500-$3,000). One-time campaign blast with results report.
- **30-day SEO + GBP teardown report** (paid audit, $500-1,500). Often converts to retainer.

### Core productized service — retainer ($2,500-$5,000/mo + setup)
- **Standard retainer**: GBP optimization + local SEO + reviews + monthly reporting + reactivation campaigns. $2,500-3,500/mo.
- **Plus paid search**: above + Google Ads management. $3,500-4,500/mo.
- **Comprehensive**: above + Meta Ads + content production + email marketing. $4,500-5,500/mo.

### Retainer / continuity (same as core) — month-to-month preferred over annual lock-in.

### High-ticket / custom ($10k+ one-time or $7k+/mo retainer)
- **Multi-location small group** (2-5 locations). Per-location pricing.
- **Practice transition** marketing (acquired practice or retiring-doctor transition).
- **Practice exit prep** — 12-month engagement to maximize collections before sale.

## Common offer structures (filled examples)

### Mid-market dental retainer offer
- **Investment:** $3,500/mo + $2,500 setup
- **Core deliverable:** GBP rebuild + local SEO + paid search + reactivation + monthly reporting
- **Dream outcome:** "Add 8-12 high-value (FFS or PPO-out-of-network) new patients per month, no Groupon-style discounting."
- **Time to first result:** 30 days (reactivation campaign + GBP optimization)
- **Time to full result:** 4-6 months
- **Effort:** 1 onboarding call + 30-min monthly review + 24-hour creative approval SLA
- **Bonuses:**
  - Done-for-you GBP rebuild — worth $1,500
  - 30-day reactivation campaign — worth $2,500
  - Custom intake form integrated with PMS — worth $1,800
  - Quarterly competitor teardown — worth $4,800/yr
  - **Total: $10,600**
- **Guarantee:** "$2,500 setup + first month. If you don't see 5+ net-new patient inquiries by day 30, refund setup, end engagement no questions asked. After day 30: month-to-month."
- **Capacity:** "We onboard 3 practices per month, first Monday. Geographic exclusivity: 1 practice per 5-mile radius. Currently 8/12 slots filled."

### Premium multi-location offer
- **Investment:** $9,500/mo + $10,000 setup
- **Core deliverable:** Multi-location SEO + GBP per location + ads management + on-call strategy
- **Dream outcome:** "Net-new patient volume aligned to chair capacity across all locations within 6 months; reduced location-level CAC by 40%."
- **Time to first result:** 60 days (per-location SEO + GBP)
- **Time to full result:** 6-9 months
- **Bonuses:**
  - Per-location GBP rebuild — worth $1,500 × N locations
  - Multi-location reporting dashboard custom-built — worth $5,000
  - Quarterly executive strategy review — worth $4,000/quarter
- **Guarantee:** "If we don't deliver location-level CAC reduction of at least 25% within 6 months, the engagement extends at no cost until we do."

## Anti-offers (don't propose these)

- **"% of new-patient revenue."** Hard to verify, leads to disputes, awkward.
- **"Per-lead pricing."** Race to the bottom on lead quality. Reject.
- **"Performance-only — no retainer."** Aggressive, high-risk. Only viable if the agency has 12+ months of dental experience and ironclad attribution.
- **"$10k for a website."** One-and-done websites don't sustain the agency. If the prospect only wants a site, refer to a peer.
- **"Free month if you sign annual."** The annual lock-in offsets the free month, but lock-ins read as desperation in 2026. Use month-to-month + month-30 guarantee instead.

## Upsells from the core retainer

After 90 days of a successful core retainer:
- **Quarterly competitor teardown report** — $1,200/quarter (already a bonus).
- **Hygienist-recruitment pipeline** — $2,500/mo to fill open hygienist positions via paid + organic recruitment marketing.
- **Patient-financing partnership content** — $1,500 one-time to integrate Sunbit / CareCredit content + landing pages.
- **Spanish-language site translation** — $4,000 one-time.
- **Custom case-study production** (video + written) — $2,500 per case study.

These upsell at 60-80% gross margin and add $2-5k MRR per client at low effort.

## Client success metrics to report

Every monthly retainer report includes:

- **Net new patient inquiries** (from all sources, attributed)
- **By source:** organic / GBP / paid search / paid social / referral / direct
- **Booked vs. inquired** (booking rate)
- **Schedule density change** (hygiene + doctor)
- **GBP performance:** views, calls, direction requests, profile actions
- **Organic ranking deltas** for tracked keywords
- **Paid search:** CPL, conversion rate, total spend, ROAS
- **Reactivation:** patients contacted, reactivated, revenue attributed

The reporting dashboard is part of the retainer — the operator doesn't manually compile these. Tooling: Plausible (web), GA4 (if client uses it), GBP API, Google Ads API, the practice's PMS for booked-vs-inquired.
