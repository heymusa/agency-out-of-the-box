# Strong Niches for Service-Business Agencies

Pre-researched niches that score well on the rubric. This is a starting library, not exhaustive. Each entry includes a starter pain-points list and a positioning angle the operator can validate.

## Healthcare practices

### Dental practices ($1M-$5M revenue)
- **Buyer:** Owner-dentist or partner-dentist (5/5 authority).
- **Deal size:** $2-5k/mo retainer (4/5).
- **Pain:** Insurance reimbursement compression, hygienist shortage, new-patient acquisition CAC up 60% post-2023.
- **Angle:** "We add 8-12 high-value (FFS or PPO-out-of-network) new patients per month, with no Groupon-style discounting."
- **Why it works:** Cluster at AGD, AAID, ADA conferences. Strong referral motion among dentists. Recurring SEO + Google Ads retainer is natural. Local SEO has clear E-E-A-T levers (Google Business Profile, reviews, NAP consistency).

### Med-spas
- Similar structure to dental but slightly higher ad-CPL volatility.
- **Angle:** "Fill your injector's chair without burning $80 CPLs on Meta — we run influencer-seeded UGC + retention email."

### Veterinary practices
- **Pain:** Corporate consolidation (VCA / Mars) pressuring independents.
- **Angle:** Independent-vet positioning + community marketing.

### Chiropractors / PT clinics
- **Pain:** Insurance billing + self-pay conversion.
- **Angle:** Cash-pay program funnel — high-ticket package sales.

## Local trades / home services

### Roofing contractors (storm-driven)
- **Buyer:** Owner (5/5).
- **Deal size:** $5k+ project, retainer optional (4/5).
- **Pain:** Storm-season demand spikes that they can't capitalize on without inbound.
- **Angle:** "Storm-ready: pre-built SEO landing pages for every zip code in your service area, ready to go live the day a storm hits."

### HVAC / plumbing / electrical
- **Pain:** Service-area boundary leakage; expensive Google Ads CPL.
- **Angle:** Local SEO + Google Business Profile dominance + service-area pages.

### Solar installers
- **Pain:** ITC tax-credit-driven seasonality; consumer trust deficit post-bankruptcies.
- **Angle:** Trust-stack landing pages with permitting transparency, BBB, real install photos.

### Custom home builders / remodelers
- **Pain:** Lead quality (browsers vs. real budgets).
- **Angle:** Qualifier-style funnel that filters $50k-vs-$500k projects pre-call.

## Legal practices

### Personal injury / car accident
- **Buyer:** Managing partner.
- **Deal size:** $10-25k/mo retainers (5/5).
- **Pain:** Severe Google Ads CPL inflation ($150-400 per click in some markets).
- **Angle:** "Stop bleeding on Google Ads — we own organic for [city] [practice area] in 9 months, then dial down ad spend by 70%."

### Family law / divorce
- **Pain:** Highly local, brand-trust-heavy.
- **Angle:** Reputation-management plus content-led trust building.

### Estate planning
- **Pain:** Boomer demographic boom; positioning vs. Trust & Will / online services.
- **Angle:** "Why boilerplate online wills fail in [state]" — local trust authority play.

## B2B fractional / consulting

### Fractional CFOs (serving Series A-B SaaS)
- **Buyer:** Founder-CEO of B2B SaaS.
- **Deal size:** $5-15k/mo retainer (5/5).
- **Pain:** Lead-gen via LinkedIn is saturated, referrals plateau at 20 clients.
- **Angle:** Outbound + thought-leadership content for fractional CFOs themselves (productized lead-gen agency for fractional executives).

### Fractional CMOs
- Similar to fractional CFO; slightly more competitive.

### B2B sales recruiters / RPO
- **Pain:** Tech downturns drying up demand, but stable mid-market.
- **Angle:** "Pre-built outbound sequences for every B2B sales role you place" — productized hiring marketing.

## Knowledge / coaching businesses

### Coaches doing $250k-$1M/yr
- **Pain:** Plateau between "DM hustle" and "actual sales team."
- **Angle:** Build them an evergreen webinar funnel + ascension to $25k mastermind.

### Course creators with audience but no funnel
- **Pain:** Big email list, weak monetization.
- **Angle:** Application funnel for high-ticket coaching attached to course.

## Fitness / wellness

### Boutique fitness studios (CrossFit boxes, F45, Pilates)
- **Pain:** Member churn 6-9 months post-signup.
- **Angle:** Onboarding-CRO + retention email sequences attached to lead-gen.

### Personal trainers $100k+
- **Pain:** Scaling 1:1 to group / online without losing margin.
- **Angle:** Productized online-coaching funnel.

## What we DON'T recommend (for solo / small team)

- **Restaurants / hospitality** — margins too thin, owners undervalue marketing, churn high.
- **Real estate agents (residential)** — too price-sensitive, CPL inflated, deal size doesn't justify.
- **E-commerce DTC** — long deserves dedicated agencies (Common Thread, etc.), needs paid-media depth.
- **Generic "small business" or "main street"** — TAM looks big but unit economics fail.
- **Crypto / web3 / NFT services** — boom-bust, regulatory risk.
- **AI startups** — over-saturated agency-side, payments unreliable from early-stage clients.

## How to use this library

When the operator describes their background, network, and skill match in Step 2, cross-reference this library and pick 3 candidates that align. Don't just pick the highest-scoring abstract niche; match it to the operator's existing affinities.
