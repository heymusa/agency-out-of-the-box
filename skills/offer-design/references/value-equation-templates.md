## 1. Dental marketing agency

**Dream outcome:** "Add 8-12 high-value (FFS or PPO out-of-network) new patients per month to your practice, without Groupon-style discounting that erodes case acceptance."

**Perceived likelihood:**
- Currently serving 14 practices across 9 states.
- Average new-patient volume increase: 47% in 90 days.
- Documented case studies from Dr. Sarah Chen (Austin), Dr. Marcus Patel (Tampa) — both willing to take reference calls.
- Founder is a former practice owner — speaks the language of case acceptance, hygiene capacity, treatment plan presentation.

**Time delay:**
- Week 1: Audit deck + Google Business Profile optimization live.
- Week 2: Reactivation campaign to your existing inactive patient list goes out.
- Week 3-4: First new patients from the reactivation start booking.
- Month 2-3: SEO + paid-search momentum building; new-patient inquiries from cold traffic.
- Month 4-6: Full system maturity; predictable 8-12/mo cadence.

**Effort & sacrifice:**
- 1 onboarding call (90 min).
- One-page asset checklist (logo, before/after photos if available, treatment menu, current patient list export).
- 30-min monthly review call.
- 24-hour approval SLA on creative.
- That's it. We handle ad management, content creation, GBP, reviews, reporting.

**Bonuses:**
- Done-for-you Google Business Profile build/optimization — worth $1,500.
- 30-day reactivation campaign to inactive patients — worth $2,500.
- Custom new-patient intake form integrated with Dentrix / Eaglesoft — worth $1,800.
- Quarterly competitor teardown report — worth $4,800/year.
- Total bonus value: $10,600.

**Guarantee:** Hybrid. Pay $2,500 setup + first month. If you don't see at least 5 net-new patient inquiries by day 30, we refund the setup fee and unsubscribe you. After day 30, you're on month-to-month — cancel anytime, no contract.

**Urgency / scarcity:**
- We onboard 3 new practices per month, first Monday of the month.
- Geographic exclusivity: one practice per 5-mile radius.
- Currently 8 of 12 slots filled.

**Investment:** $3,500/mo + $2,500 one-time setup.

## How to use these templates

These are scaffolding. The operator's job — and your job during `offer-design` — is to substitute real numbers, real client names, real bonus value anchors, and a real guarantee structure that the operator will defend in a pricing call.

**Never paste a template as-is into agency-context.md.** Always re-engineer with the operator's specifics.
